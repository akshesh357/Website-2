ACCESSORIES

CUE TIPS

Add to cart

Made from a specially treated compressed leather, these cue tips are of the highest quality and used by many top professional players. They are impregnated with chalk, giving the cue tip higher consistency which in turn reduces miscuing and allows for more grip and spin on the cue ball. Available in your choice of size from 8mm up to 13mm, and in pack sizes 5,10, 25 and box of 50.

CHALKS

Add to cart

Pioneer Pool or Snooker Cue Chalk, designed for the more budget conscious player, Pioneer cue chalk offers outstanding value for money. Pioneer specialise in economical chalk production; perfect for beginners or leisure players. Available in 12 pack, 36 pack and 1 gross (144 pieces), and a wide range of colours. Ideal Use: Perfect for commercial use in pubs and clubs where the turnover of chalk is high and also very suitable as a great starter chalk for kids tables. Vast array of colours make choosing one for your cloth colour quite easy.

NYLON SNOOKER OR POOL TABLE BRUSH

Add to cart

Nylon table brush with bristles. Ideal for removing chalk and dust from your table cloth. Regular brushing is very important in terms of pool or snooker table cloth life and will also help to make the nap run in the correct direction. For those hard to reach areas, use the 8" rail brush to get under the rail where chalk and dust easily gather. Also available: 7" and 12" table brush.
